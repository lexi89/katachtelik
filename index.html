I'm Kat — a copywriter with 5 years of experience in direct digital marketing, currently based in London.


Work stuff.

I had a chance to learn and grow in cross-culture teams, ranging from 4-pax start-up to 200-pax international company. In that time I created a lush potpourri of copy — sales pages, content emails, funnels, video scripts, product descriptions, blog posts... You name it, I've probably done it. I believe copywriting is both science and art. A good copywriter should be creative AND scientific, producing exciting copy and results. Scroll down to see my portfolio and some referrals.

Other stuff


As cheesy as it sounds, I'm passionate about... life, in totality of its majesty and lunacy.

I'm a Yoga Teacher, a foodie and a committed advocate of personal growth, movement and healthy lifestyle. I'm also a travel junkie, an amateur photographer and David Attenborough admirer.

Because I believe that "what goes around, comes around", whenever possible I get involved in (hands-on or digital) volunteer projects. Some of them focus on animal welfare, whereas other on human causes — like women empowerment through micro-loans (India) or coaching for underprivileged refugee kids (Malaysia). For more details on these, check out my LinkedIn profile.
