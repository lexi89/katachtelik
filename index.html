Hi, I'm Kat.


Thanks for stopping by.

I'm a copywriter with 5 years of experience in direct digital marketing, currently based in London.


Work stuff.

I had a chance to learn and grow in cross-culture teams, ranging from 4-pax start-up to 200-pax international company. In that time I created a lush potpourri of copy — sales pages, content emails, funnels, video scripts, product descriptions, blog posts... You name it, I've probably done it.

I believe copywriting is both science and art. A good copywriter should be creative AND scientific, producing exciting copy and results.

Other stuff
